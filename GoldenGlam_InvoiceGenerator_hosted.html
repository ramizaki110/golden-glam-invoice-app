📋 How to generate an invoice — step by step
Step 1 — Fill in the invoice
  1. Go to the New Invoice tab (left sidebar)
  2. Fill in Invoice details — invoice number, date, and optional reference
  3. Select or fill in the Client details
  4. Click + Add item for each product — fill in description, item no., qty, price, discount, and optionally upload a product photo
  5. Set delivery charge, sales tax rate, delivery type and payment terms
  6. Add any notes to appear on the invoice (e.g. fixture bulb disclaimers)
Step 2 — Generate the summary
  1. Go to the Summary & Export tab (left sidebar)
  2. Click Refresh to generate the summary block
  3. Click Copy to copy it to your clipboard
Step 3 — Send to Claude
  1. Open a new Claude chat
  2. Upload golden_glam_invoice_generator.py and golden_glam_logo_final.png
  3. Paste the copied summary and send with the message:
    Generate the PDF invoice from this summary using my script
Step 4 — Download both output files

Claude always produces two files — download both every time:

📄 PDF — Client invoice
GG__SI<NO>_<CLIENT>.pdf
📊 Excel — Internal report
GG__SI<NO>_<CLIENT>_INTERNAL.xlsx
Cost · Profit · Margin · Vendor nos.
